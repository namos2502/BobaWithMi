WHO WE ARE

A SANCTUARY OF FLAVOURS

At Boba With Mi, we're more than just a bubble tea cafe; we're a premium destination where traditional meets today. Nestled in the heart of Hamilton Brisbane, our sanctuary is dedicated to the art of bubble tea, offering a retreat for both aficionados and newcomers alike. Our passion for quality, from the finest teas to the freshest ingredients, crafts not just a drink, but an experience. Welcome to the temple where every sip tells a story.

WHAT WE OFFER

CURATED WITH CARE

Immerse yourself in our varied menu, where each bubble tea creation has been prepared with premium, handpicked ingredients. Whether you are enjoying traditional classics or innovative Boba With Mi specialties, our celebrated milk teas, invigorating fruit teas, and inventive brews cater to all. Indulgence at Boba With Mi is all about attention to detail and a commitment to quality.
