WHO WE ARE

A FAMILY TRADITION

Boba With Mi is a family-owned Vietnamese street food and milk tea shop, bringing authentic flavors from Vietnam to Falls Church. Our family has been crafting traditional Vietnamese dishes and perfecting the art of milk tea with passion and dedication. We combine authentic Vietnamese recipes with the warmth of Vietnamese hospitality. Every bowl of pho, every sip of milk tea, carries the love and heritage of our Vietnamese roots.

WHAT WE OFFER

AUTHENTIC VIETNAMESE FLAVORS

Discover the vibrant tastes of Vietnam at our family-owned street food and milk tea shop. Our menu features traditional Vietnamese dishes like banh mi, and spring rolls, prepared with authentic ingredients and family recipes. Complement your meal with our signature milk teas, crafted with premium tea leaves and fresh ingredients. From classic Vietnamese coffee to innovative boba creations, every item reflects our commitment to bringing genuine Vietnamese street food culture to our community.

HOW WE SEE THE WORLD

FAMILY VALUES & SUSTAINABILITY

As a family-owned Vietnamese business, we honor our heritage by caring for our community and the environment. We use biodegradable packaging and source ingredients responsibly, reflecting the Vietnamese tradition of living in harmony with nature. Our commitment to sustainability goes hand-in-hand with our family values of quality, authenticity, and community. Every choice we make honors both our Vietnamese roots and our responsibility to future generations.
